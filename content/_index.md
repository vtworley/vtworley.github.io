        My dissertation, *Vertical Litigation, Political Polarization, and State Attorneys General*, examines vertical litigation between the federal government and the states during the contemporary era of partisan polarization. When and why do states sue the federal government, and which states do so most aggressively? How does polarization filter into the dockets of the federal courts?
